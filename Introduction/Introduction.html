Welcome to Cybertitan EDU

Cybertitan EDU is a side project developed by a team captain in the Canadian Cybertitan competition. This whole website is meant to be the foundation of resources and stratagies to hopefully qualify for competitions. This project is made for members and people who want to learn or have a look at what Cybertitan is about. This project began in early July and has been going strong ever since.

Cybersecurity and Cybertitans

Cybertitans is about cybersecurity and how you can develop and find fixes for issues on a computer. It's all virtual and set up by the American Air Force. What's so great about competiting is solving with your team and facing challenges. The whole competition does a really great job at giving everyone an authentic experience. Cybersecurity does demand a few things, which scales with how much you are willing to put into it. I found Cybersecurity amuzing and great to tinker around with. Others find it fun to be around the people. It's based around Cybersecurity but it's much more.

After the 24-25 Season

After the 24-25 season I would have to say that it has been a success. The amount of pure information and learning that we have done is something that could never to traded. It's unfortunate that we couldn't make it to semis and according to the sheets, we were still 53 points off from making it. Even with such a great year we still needed a little bit more. We have a couple of more teams next year that will hopefully take everything from this year and improve.

Our results really show everything we were the best in New Brunswick with our second team following us by 64 points. If I could give any advice to anyone next year doing Cybertitans, it's to never quit and keep pushing thourhg even it's a huge pit of learning it's all worth it in the end!
