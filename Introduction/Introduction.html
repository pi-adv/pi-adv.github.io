Introduction

This section is all about teaching you the very basics of cybersecurity, but also how the Cybertitans setup and compete.

What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity put simply is the way of securing some electronic from being hacked from something that is not authorized. This is the foundation of Cybersecurity, protecting what we have and keeping the people that shouldn't have it, out.

If I were to ask, what's the first thing that comes to mind when asking to protect an account? Most would say a password. Already if you have passwords on any of your accounts, you have practiced cybersecurity in a way.

This is fantastic because to learn the next steps, you are not only making passwords, but building a strong wall to keep people out and away from getting it.

What is the Cybertitans?

The cybertitans is a competition that has been running for over a decade. Teams are signed up and compete in competitions over the course of the year to try and win the finals. The teams range across Canada being seperated in two catagories. 1. Middle School, 2. High School/Open. We compete in the High School/Open which means we compete against every other High School and any team that is in the Open division.

We compete in at least 3 competitions.

  1. Round 1 (24-25 Season --> (October 24-27))
  2. Round 2 (24-25 Season --> (November 14-17))
  3. State Round (24-25 Season --> (December 12-15))

These 3 comptitions are considered as the QUALIFYING rounds. Since your points are combined to see if you qualify for the next few rounds.

  1. SemiFinals (24-25 Season --> (January 23-25))
  2. National Finals (24-25 Season --> (March 14-18))

The other rounds will only take a number of teams from each league.
