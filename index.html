Welcome to the official unofficial CyberTraining for the OHS Cybertitan Team. In this website you will find many different tools and important learning tutorials to help you setup for competition.

A Breakdown of the Options

  • Introduction

    • This page is for the newbies who haven't had previous years of experience. This gives a very simple explanation of how the cybertitan teams and competitions work.

  • HYPERNOVA

    • This is page is the gold mine where I found Hybernova's blog that documents everything that we need to do. EVERYTHING.

  • Chapters

    • This page is the whole content for this site. Each Chapter will explain a certain part of the proceedure that's recommended for you to learn and use during competition.

  • Tools (Out of Competition)

    • This page is about different tools you can use to prepare for competition rather than using them during. These tools will be ones that you will need to pay attention to and are recommeneded to take notes from these tools to take those into competition.

  • Tools (Inside Competition)

    • This page includes tools that you can use during competitions. This ranges from applications, websites and even crucial notes that might help when you are in a pinch.

  • The Teams

    • This page is a brief discription of the teams in the 2024-2025 season and few all star teams from years past.
